About The Event

About The Event

A panel discussion, where we will have eminent and successful personalities and entrepreneurs, who can enlighten and share their journey to the conclave of their career with the young and innovative minds! The panelists are going to be from diverse domains: Science & Technology, Farming & Agriculture, Healthcare, Fashion & Art legal & Patent rights, etc. The discussion will be regarding the emerging entrepreneurs, the entrepreneurship in India and around the globe. What does it take to make your business a success? How to balance between the nadir and zenith of the business life? All your questions will be discussed on this platform!
